# Competitive scan

Read `../../CONVENTIONS.md` (at the plugin root, relative to this skill's directory) first.

## Scope

Confirm: competitor(s) or feature area, and depth (quick scan ~15 min of research vs. deep brief). Default to quick scan.

## Research

Web search: competitor site + docs + changelog/release notes, pricing pages, recent press/blog posts, community discussion (Reddit, HN, forums), job postings (signals of investment areas). Also search internal sources — Confluence for past battlecards, Slack for field anecdotes from the team — and mark internal vs. external evidence.

## Output

1. **One-paragraph positioning read** — how they want to be perceived vs. how the market talks about them.
2. **Capability comparison** — table against our relevant product area(s): feature, them, us, gap direction. Only include rows where evidence exists; mark unknowns as unknowns.
3. **Pricing & packaging signals** — with links.
4. **Recent moves** (last 2 quarters) — launches, hires, partnerships.
5. **Implications for ACX** — 3-4 sentences: where we're exposed, where we can attack, what to watch.
6. **Sources** — every claim linked.

Be evenhanded; a brief that flatters us is useless. Show the draft, then ask the output-destination question per conventions.
